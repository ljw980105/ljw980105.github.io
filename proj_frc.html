    Head Programmer

    Since my inception in robotics back in 2013, I have always been the go-to person when it comes to programming the robot. I taught coding to a group of 20 students, established my foundation in programming with Java, and led the programing team to code 3 perfectly functional robots with two of them ranking top 10 in FRC NYC competition. In April 2016, I won the MVP award given by FRC Long Island.

    Videos, Videos, and Videos

    During these 3 years, I've also developed a firm interest in video production. As a result, I've filmed and produced professional videos documenting the building season and the competitions, accumulating over 20,000 views.I've also mastered video editing software including Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects as well as Adobe Audition.
